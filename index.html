Introduction

You're here because you know me or you would like to get to know me. Hi there! My name is Albert and I am currently living and working in the beautiful city of Chicago. I love exploring cities and college campuses, and besides Chicago, have spent considerable time in Hong Kong, Toronto, and Atlanta. I am both a Northwestern and Georgia Tech alum, but my heart is still with the Wildcats.

Web Applications

I enjoy creating open-source web applications. Lately, my toolkit has mostly been AngularJS and Rails, with MongoDB or PostgreSQL database. With AngularJS, integration with MongoDB is seamless through the MEAN stack. Rails and Postgres work well together especially with Heroku applications, so that's also been a recent focus of mine. I hope to eventually explore Ember.js and Python/Django, and I'm intrigued by Julia as a potential competitor to Python and R, as well as Swift, the future of iOS development.
